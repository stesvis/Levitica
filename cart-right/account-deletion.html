Account Deletion Request

We're sorry to see you go. If you would like to delete your Cart Right account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to the address below with the subject line "Delete my account". Please include the email address associated with your Cart Right account in the body of the message.

levitica.apps@gmail.com

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account and profile information
  • All shopping lists you created
  • Your membership in any shared lists
  • All associated app data

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Your data will be removed within 30 days of your request.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the deletion process, please contact us at levitica.apps@gmail.com.
